QUITLY

Quitly is an app for anyone who has a bad habit they would like to quit. Quitly lets you quickly add your own custom habit, like over-eating to keep track of how long you have gone without. Use our forum to get help from people struggling with similar habits or to just vent about anything. Click learn more to see how the app works!

THE PROBLEM

Habits and addiction can be tough to deal with, especially when there is stigma surrounding these issues. Habits also affects people in different ways, since everyone is different. Quitting any habit is also tough when you don’t get the support you need. Learn more about the problem we are trying to solve by clicking under.

THE SOLUTION

Quitly helps you quit any habit you want, by making adding you habits a feature in the app. This could be anything, and as more users use the app, the probability of someone quitting the same habit as you increases. Supporting each other is a huge factor in quitting a habit, which is why the forum section in Quitly is extremely valuable.
